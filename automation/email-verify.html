📚 Email Verification Tips

  • Always verify emails before sending cold outreach
  • Invalid/risky emails waste time and hurt your sender reputation
  • This tool checks format, domain, and patterns (not server-based)
  • Red flags: Disposable emails (temp-mail), obvious typos, inactive domains
  • Green flags: Professional domain emails, proper format, active business domains
  • Use verified emails to improve campaign response rates
  • Add verified emails to your outreach campaigns
