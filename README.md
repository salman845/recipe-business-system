# 🍳 Recipe Business System - Complete Client Acquisition Toolkit

A comprehensive system for food recipe developers to attract premium clients, manage leads, and scale their business from $0 → $5K+ monthly revenue.

## 📦 What's Included

### 1. **Lead Tracker App** 
Web-based CRM dashboard to manage all your prospects, follow-ups, and deals.
- **Location:** `lead-tracker/` 
- **Features:** Track prospects, manage pipeline, generate reports
- **Access:** Open `lead-tracker/index.html` in your browser

### 2. **Portfolio Website**
Professional website showcasing your recipe services and expertise.
- **Location:** `portfolio/`
- **Features:** Service showcase, case studies, contact form
- **Access:** Open `portfolio/index.html` in your browser (or deploy to GitHub Pages)

### 3. **Outreach Manager**
Tracking system for managing emails, LinkedIn messages, WhatsApp pitches, and follow-ups.
- **Location:** `outreach-manager/`
- **Features:** Campaign tracking, response logging, analytics
- **Access:** Open `outreach-manager/index.html` in your browser

### 4. **Strategy Document**
Complete 90-day action plan with templates, scripts, and implementation guides.
- **Location:** `STRATEGY.md`
- **Features:** Ideal client profiles, outreach templates, pricing packages, daily action steps

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Clone or download this repository**
2. **Open any `.html` file in your browser** to use the apps (no installation needed)
3. **Read `STRATEGY.md`** for detailed business guidance
4. **Start with the Lead Tracker** to begin managing prospects

## 💼 How to Use This System

### Week 1: Setup & Foundation
- [ ] Customize the portfolio website with your info
- [ ] Fill in your ideal client profiles in the strategy doc
- [ ] Add your first 10 prospects to the Lead Tracker

### Week 2-4: Lead Generation
- [ ] Use outreach templates from `STRATEGY.md`
- [ ] Track all outreach in the Outreach Manager
- [ ] Log leads in the Lead Tracker
- [ ] Follow up using the 90-day plan

### Week 5+: Optimization
- [ ] Review analytics in Lead Tracker
- [ ] Refine messaging based on response rates
- [ ] Scale successful outreach channels
- [ ] Close deals and request testimonials
